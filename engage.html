1. What is Evolution anyways?

Learning Objectives

Students will think about what they already know and compare evolution in Pokemon to a real-life situation.

In this video you see the evolution of bacteria over a period of time. At first, the bacteria were not able to live on the anti-bacterial surfaces, but over time, they could! This is because of process of evolution through random mutations and selection. Even though the original bacteria couldn't originally survive in an extreme environment, some of their decendents could.

While bacteria aren't exactly the same as Pokemon, the same principles of evolution apply.
