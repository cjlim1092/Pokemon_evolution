5. Evolution in the Alola Region

Learning Objectives

Students will think about how traits fit with their environment.

When the Alola region was introduced, a whole new world of tropical pokemon were revealed. Some of these pokemon were similar to pokemon we have seen before, but with slight differences. This is because of natural selection. Natural selection is the process where individuals who are better adapted to their envrionment tend to survive and produce more offspring. Thus these adaptations will be passed on over time. For example, a polar bear has fur which is thick and white. The thick fur was an adaptation for the cold environment. The white fur was another adaptation that helped them live in snowy areas.
