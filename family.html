Pikachu Family

Learning Goals:

    Offspring Inherit traits from their parents, but are ot identical to their parents
    There is variation within a population

This is a family photo of the pikachu family. There is a Mom, a Dad, and a baby. The baby looks like the Mom and the Dad. This is because the baby inherited traits from each parent.

Looking at the picture, determine which parent the baby got each trait from

Optional: Take a look at a family photo with your parents. Determine which parent passed on each trait to you.

Do children look exactly like their parents? Why or why not?

But wait! There are also traits that children might have that neither of their parents had. These are called mutations and they come from the process of combining the DNA from each parent

  • Sometimes these mutations can not be seen and do not make a difference
  • Sometimes these mutations can be seen, and help the children survive to pass on these traits to their children
  • Sometimes these mutations hurt the child, and so the child does not survive and so it can't pass them on to future children.
