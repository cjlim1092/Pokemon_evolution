2. What is Metamorphosis?

Learning Objectives

Students will metamorphosis in nature to evolution in Pokemon games.


Metamorphosis is the process of transformation from an immature form to an adult form in two or more distinct stages. Basically it is the way that some animals grow up. It is not evolution because it happens to one individual, not an entire species.
